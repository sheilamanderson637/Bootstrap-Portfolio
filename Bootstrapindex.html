Sheila Anderson Bio

I am a Coding Bootcamp student at Northwestern University. I live in the Northwest suburbs of Chicago.

I have extensive business experience working for a major housewares retail company holding positions as Sales Audit Manager, Store Operations Project Manager and Manager of the Customer Store Experience. In these roles, I served as project manager, stakeholder and business expert for many internally developed software programs including; POS, cash handling, an internal sales aid website, and various operations apps.

I am fascinated with front end web design and elements that make the user experience intuitive and engaging. I hope to work on the front end of web design.
